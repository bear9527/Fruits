About us

Shanghai thousand grains co., LTD., founded in 2015, belongs to the South African diamond corporation, as a whole to domestic and foreign agricultural products, food and agricultural capital goods sales, trade and investment, become a group of agricultural strategic management platform.

The company mainly deals with the sales, trade and investment of agricultural products, food and agricultural products at home and abroad, which is the strategic management platform of the group. The company's business has developed rapidly, and has successfully operated the international trade business of South Africa and the world's major fruit producing areas, and has established a stable sales network with major domestic channels.
